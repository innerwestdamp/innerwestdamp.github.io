Damp Problems

If you've noticed peeling paint, mould problems or musty odours you probably have a damp problem. We are specialists in the two major causes of damp problems - rising damp and condensation.

Professional Solutions

We can diagnose the underlying cause of your damp before any treatment begins and clearly explain what needs to happen. We have a wide range of professional solutions available including air vents, fan ventilation and wall restorations.

Friendly and Professional Service

We have over 30 years experience in the industry - as well as certification on carpentry with extensive knowledge of building and structure. This knowledge can be invaluable when diagnosing problems associated with damp.
