How Do You Know If You Have a Problem With Rising Damp?


Many people are unaware they have a damp problem. Common symptoms include:

  • Peeling paint
  • Flaking plaster
  • Mortar between bricks falling out
  • Musty odours
  • Condensation on windows
If you've noticed any of these issues, contact Inner West Damp now for your free quote.

Damp and mould can also cause health problems such as allergies, asthma and sinus problems. We can inspect your property and diagnose the underlying cause of your damp problems, leaving you with the peace of mind that you and your family are safe and healthy.


What Are The Solutions to Rising Damp?


There are two ways to combat damp problems. Stop the damp from rising, and/or increase ventilation.

Sub-floor Ventilation

Improved sub – floor ventilation allows clean fresh air to circulate under your floor to help reduce musty odours inside the home. Upgrading of the existing vents is an inexpensive and effective means of keeping damp to a minimum at sub – floor level.

The Benefits of passive ventilation

  • Environmentally friendly
  • No running costs
  • Allows light in to deter termites
  • Cheaper to install
