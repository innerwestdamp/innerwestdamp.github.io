How Do You Know If You Have a Problem With Rising Damp?

Many people are unaware they have a damp problme. Common symptoms include:

  • Peeling paint
  • Flaking plaster
  • Mortar between bricks falling out
  • Musty odours
  • Condensation on windows
If you've noticed any of these issues, contact Inner West Damp now for your free quote.

Damp and mould can also cause health problems such as allergies, asthma and sinus problems. We can inspect your property and diagnose the underlying cause of your damp problems.
